About ReceiptToCSV

ReceiptToCSV is a simple, powerful tool designed to streamline your expense management. Our AI-powered platform extracts data from your receipts and converts it into clean CSV files — no hassle, no complexity.

We believe in simplicity: no signups required and no ads cluttering your experience. Just upload your receipts, get your CSV, and move on.

Whether you're tracking business expenses or managing personal budgets, ReceiptToCSV is here to help you save time and stay organized.
