What is ReceiptToCSV?

Cash free, login free, and ad free way to upload your receipt photos and get back useful data in a spreadsheet format emailed to you, or downloadable. Get back itemized line breakdown reports of your receipts, as well as the totals, tax, store, and other information.

Powerful Features

Line-by-Line Item Extraction

Extract every individual item with prices and quantities

Financial Totals

Capture subtotal, tax amounts, tips, and final totals

Payment Method Detection

Identify cash, card, or other payment methods used

CSV Export

Download clean, organized spreadsheet files

Email Delivery

Get results sent directly to your inbox

Privacy First

No accounts, no tracking, data processed securely

How It Works

Upload Your Receipt
