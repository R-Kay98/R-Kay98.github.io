Free Online Receipt Scanner

ReceiptToCSV is a free online receipt scanner web app built for small businesses and individuals who want a fast and private way to extract data from receipts. Unlike bulky expense-tracking platforms, ReceiptToCSV focuses purely on one thing: accurate receipt scanning. No logins, no accounts, and no tracking — just upload your receipt and get structured results in seconds.

Powered by advanced AI and OCR (Optical Character Recognition), our tool automatically detects and extracts line-by-line items, subtotal, tax amounts, total cost, and payment method. Whether you're scanning grocery receipts, business purchases, or service invoices, ReceiptToCSV works across a wide range of printed and digital formats.

Once scanned, your data can be exported to a clean spreadsheet or CSV file, making it easy to organize, analyze, or share with your accountant. You can even have the results emailed to you for quick access and record-keeping.

ReceiptToCSV is available on the web, as well as through the App Store and Google Play — and it works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices. Designed for simplicity and speed, it's the ideal tool for anyone who needs accurate receipt data without the hassle of signing up or dealing with ads.

No login or credit card information is required to use this service. Just load the web page or app and get started instantly.
